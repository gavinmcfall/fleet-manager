Layout rules:
Gimballed = 2 rows: mount row (VariPuck + gimbal icon + WG) then child row (└ bracket + weapon + stats).
Fixed = 1 row: weapon sits at parent level (fixed icon + "fixed" badge + WG), stats on second line right-aligned. No bracket, no child indentation.

Interactions:
Gimbal mount row: click to open picker → swap to a different mount type (fixed, gimbal) or remove the mount entirely.
Weapon child row: click to open picker → swap the weapon on this mount.
Fixed weapon row: click to open picker → swap the weapon, or switch to a gimbal mount (weapon size -1).
Weapon group badges (1-4): click to toggle assignment to fire groups.

Icons: = Gimballed (circle + crosshair — rotational freedom)   = Fixed (square + crosshair — locked, no articulation)

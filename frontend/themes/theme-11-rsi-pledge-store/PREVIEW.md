# Theme 11: RSI Pledge Store

The official RSI website aesthetic. Dark navy backgrounds, warm orange accents for CTAs and badges, steel blue for secondary elements and navigation. Clean, modern, premium. The look players see when spending real money on ships.

## Special Effects
- **Solid orange buttons:** btn-primary uses solid bg instead of transparent, with orange box-shadow
- **Steel blue headers:** Panel headers use blue secondary instead of gray
- **Warm shadows:** Cards have subtle dark box-shadow for depth
- **Rounded corners:** Uses standard rounded-lg (matches RSI's modern feel)
- **No glow/scanline effects:** Clean and professional like the real RSI site
